Kodumaro :: Saluton!

The shadows behind the code.

Hi there!

Kodumaro is a colaboration between three friend programmers: Cláudio Torcato, Rodrigo Cacilhas, e Walter Cruz, in an effort to offer you some interesting tips about programming.

The topics vary from beginner to pro, from basic programming concepts to severial different language samples, in a light and easy wording.

We hope the posts could be useful and enlightening, and we’re waiting for your return, in order to improve Kodumaro everyday.

Without further ado, be presented your medium: Kodumaro!
