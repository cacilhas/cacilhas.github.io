Kodumaro :: Why Lombok is a waste of time

Released on January 23rd, 2019
The shadows behind the code.

I‚Äôve recently started working with Lombok, a really great project.

Let‚Äôs look at a made-easier Java code. First without Lombok (I‚Äôm suppressing the imports):
